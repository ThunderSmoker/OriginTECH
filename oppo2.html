ONLINE INTERNSHIP

Opportunity

We offer an exceptional online internship program for students pursuing a degree and diploma in E&TC, Electrical, CSE, and faculty members who want to expand their knowledge and skill set. Our program covers a wide range of topics like IoT, AWS, PLC Automation and many more.
We have designed our internship program to provide a comprehensive learning experience that combines theoretical concepts with real-world applications. You will be given access to a variety of learning resources, including recorded video lectures, online quizzes, and assignments. Our experienced industry experts will guide you throughout the program and help you gain a deep understanding of the subject matter.
At the end of the program, you will receive a Certificate of Completion, Internship Certificate and Letter of Recommendation that will demonstrate your expertise and knowledge in the field.
